CricSpin

Game Rules

Objective: Score more runs than the opponent to win.

Toss: Team 1 calls Head or Tail. Winner chooses to bat or bowl.

Gameplay: Click "Play" to spin the wheel. The arrow indicates the result.

Runs: 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 6 - Added to team and batter score.

Wide: +1 extra run, ball not counted.

No Ball: +1 extra run, ball not counted. Next ball is Free Hit (only Run Out counts).

Leg By: +1 extra run, ball counted.

Outs: Catch, Bowled, Stumped, Run Out, Hit Out - Wicket falls, batter changes.

Bowler: Changes automatically after 6 legal balls.

Innings End: When all overs are done or all wickets are lost.

Tie: Super Over - 1 over, 2 wickets. New toss required.
